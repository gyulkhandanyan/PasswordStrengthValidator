About this site

Check Password Strength on Password Strength Validator (PSV)

PSV is cool: it is an online tool that scans your password and then displays security information for your input.

PSV is safe: it doesn't share your input with a third party, so it allows you to check the passwords securely.

PSV is accurate: it calculates the password strength according to factors such as password length, use of numbers, lowercase/uppercase characters, special characters, and avoids repetitions.
